ONOF

One Nation One Funding

ONOF is a initiative to provide students/organisations a platform to solve some of the pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving.

Process Flow

Connecting funding agencies with institutions/individuals under a single portal to prevent the hassle of multiple distributed and disorganized funding.

Step 1

Problem statements submission by various Central &State Funding Agencies and Departments.

Step 2

Submission of Proposals by Institutions and Individuals on ONOF portal

Step 3

Result Analysis and Sending Sanction Letters to by evaluators
